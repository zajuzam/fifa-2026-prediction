How points are scored
Points are awarded after each match result is confirmed.
PredictionPoints
🎯 Exact score (e.g. predicted 2–1, actual 2–1) 3 pts
✅ Correct result (win/draw/loss, wrong score) 1 pt
❌ Wrong result 0 pts
Example: Real score is 2–1. You predicted 2–1 → 3 points. Your friend predicted 1–0 → 1 point (got the win right). Another friend predicted 0–2 → 0 points.
